FAQ


Q: Does Fun Fact of the Day ever take breaks?
A: Yes. FFOTD takes hiatuses when the Ithaca City School District does (i.e. Winter Break, Summer, etc.)

Q: How do I unsubscribe?
A: Because FFOTD's emails are organized into a Google Group, there will be an "Unsubscribe" button underneath my name in every email.

Q: How can I prevent these emails from clogging up my inbox?
A: If you enjoy the emails you can make them fall under your forums tab. To do this you go to settings, click configure inbox, and then check the box next to forums.

Q: Does Fun Fact of the Day have any gaming community relations?
A: Yes, we actually have a sponsor called Jebhook which provides gaming enhancement services. Their website can be found under our contacts page.
